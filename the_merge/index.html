The Merge

This is an exciting time for the Ethereum ecosystem as the Merge is coming up. What is "The Merge"? It is the transition of Ethereum moving from Proof-of-Work to a Proof-of-Stake consensus. This should lead to more scalability, sustainability, and security of the Ethereum ecosystem.
In the section below you can find more resources providing information on the upcoming upgrade - The Merge.
