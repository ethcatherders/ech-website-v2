Arrow Glacier

This is a planned upgrade to delay the difficulty bomb to be noticeable in Summer 2022.
  • Mainnet upgrade date - Wednesday, 8th of December
  • Planned for epoch - 13773000
  • EIP-4345: Difficulty Bomb Delay to June 2022
  • ECH explainer - Ethereum Arrow Glacier Upgrade
  • Ethereum Foundation's Arrow Glacier Upgrade Announcement
To understand how the “Difficulty Bomb” works in the Ethereum blockchain, please follow the recording of PEEPanEIP #39: Difficulty bomb & EIP-3554 with James Hancock.

Altair Upgrade

This is the first planned network upgrade for the Beacon chain (Ethereum 2.0). It is already activated on the Prater testnet. Follow the consensus monitor shared by Alex Stokes.
  • Mainnet upgrade date - Wednesday, 27th of October
  • Planned for epoch - 74240
  • Expected time - 10:56:23 UTC
  • Ethereum Foundation's Altair Mainnet Announcement
